Vaesen

Vaesen is a tabletop role-playing game set in a dark, mysterious version of 19th-century Scandinavia, where players take on the roles of locals investigating supernatural creatures known as "vaesen." Blending folklore and horror, the game emphasizes storytelling and collaborative problem-solving as characters confront their fears, explore haunting mysteries, and grapple with the moral dilemmas posed by the otherworldly beings in their world.

- Vaesen Character Sheet

Daggerheart

Daggerheart is a tabletop role-playing game that immerses players in a gritty, high-stakes fantasy setting, where they embody daring heroes navigating treacherous landscapes and confronting formidable foes. With a focus on character-driven storytelling and tactical gameplay, players must forge alliances, make tough choices, and harness their unique abilities to overcome the challenges that lie ahead.
