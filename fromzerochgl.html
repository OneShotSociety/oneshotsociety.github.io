D&D From Zero

Cheltenham - Space Available!

Players will learn how to create their very own D&D character, exploring the fundamentals of character creation, including race, class, and backstory. 

Beginners who have experience in making their own characters are also welcome to attend, you may just have to have a little patience whilst others complete their character sheets.

Once your characters are ready, we’ll embark on a short, engaging adventure, allowing participants to put their new skills to the test in a fun and supportive environment.

Available to Chelt/glos groups only!
