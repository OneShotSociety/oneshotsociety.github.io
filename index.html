About Us

Are you looking to try new systems without fully committing?  Are you sick of missing sessions due to scheduling issues? Are you just looking to get some extra games in or test things out before putting them in your existing campaigns?

The One Shot Society is looking to solve all these problems by hosting only one off game sessions, with no set schedule or set location everyone gets a chance to play games using different systems, experiencing a range of content from pre written supplements to a GM's own homebrew scenario.
