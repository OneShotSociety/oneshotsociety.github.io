Pop-Up Adventure

27/04 - Cardiff - Space Available!

Time to bring your favourite character or whip up a new one and be ready for anything. You could be facing any foe, uncovering any deed or crawling any dungeon in a classic one shot DnD adventure.

If you're looking to get in extra sessions or test out new characters, as long as you know the basics of DnD, then Pop-Up Adventures will be great for you.

This adventure uses material from 'Grim Hollow' and will be a similar tone of adventure.
